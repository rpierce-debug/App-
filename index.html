Coping skills

Ground yourself

Try the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding exercise to reconnect with the present.

  1. Notice five things you can see.
  2. Notice four things you can touch.
  3. Notice three things you can hear.
  4. Notice two things you can smell.
  5. Notice one thing you can taste.

Breathing

Box breathing

Use a simple rhythm to calm your nervous system.

Inhale for 4
Hold for 4
Exhale for 4
Hold for 4

Repeat for 4 rounds. Cerina will remind you if enabled above.
